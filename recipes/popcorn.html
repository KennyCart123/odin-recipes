Popcorn

Description

Popcorn is extremely easy to make, and extremely easy to combine with anything you have in the cupboard.

Cheese popcorn is no exception, and is no waste of time!

Ingredients

  • 100 Grams of Popcorn (Serves 4)
  • 3 grams Parmesan Cheese
  • 2 tbps sea salt
  • 50 ml olive OR vegetable oil.

Steps

  1. Preheat stove for around 1-2 minutes.
  2. Pour 50ml of oil in the saucepan, and wait a further 2 minutes.
  3. Place 2 or 3 kernals inside the pan and wait for all 3 to pop.
  4. After they've popped, pour 100 grams of popcorn kernals inside the pan, making sure the lid is secure.
  5. When popping is occuring between 3-5 seconds later, take pan off of stove.
  6. Remove lid, add both 2 tbps of sea salt, and 3 grams of Parmesan by crushing it over.
  7. Secure lid, and shake for around 5 seconds.
